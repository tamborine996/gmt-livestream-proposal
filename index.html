03

Questions & answers

The reasoning behind the recommendation, addressed directly.

Why do we need live streaming?

Live streaming events would mean that women and those unable to attend — such as the elderly — can partake in the benefits of religious events, increasing access across the community.

A high-quality, professional stream also enhances the reputation of GMT more broadly, helping to attract speakers, donations and wider engagement.

Why is this investment required?

Currently, Qasim's phone is relied upon to live stream using 5G. The problems with this are:

  • The sound input on Qasim's phone is broken — there has been only one live stream in 2025 and none so far in 2026.
  • 5G gives unstable quality, particularly as more attendees arrive towards the end of an event.
  • Qasim could be away or unavailable, creating a dependency on one individual that should be avoided.

Using a dedicated camera over a broadband connection, with equipment kept at the masjid, resolves all three issues.

Why has the PTZOptics camera been chosen?

This choice was made after detailed research and comparison against both cheaper and more expensive alternatives. The recommendation rests on three considerations: suitability, reliability and value for money.

1

Suitability

It does exactly what we need: 1080p streaming, 20× optical zoom, remote pan/tilt/zoom, preset positions (mimbar, wide hall, lecture), wall or ceiling mounting, and flexible connections. We deliberately avoided paying extra for 4K — 1080p is ample for phones, tablets and laptops.

2

Reliability

PTZOptics is a recognised brand across livestreaming, education, corporate AV and house-of-worship markets. The 5-year warranty gives confidence for regular use at Jumu'ah, Ramadan, classes and events — reducing the risk of an unknown-brand camera with uncertain support.

3

Value for money

It sits in the right middle ground. Cheaper cameras compromise on warranty, software and durability; broadcast-level models add cost for features a mosque stream won't use. This camera gives the features we actually need from a respected brand — without paying for the rest.
